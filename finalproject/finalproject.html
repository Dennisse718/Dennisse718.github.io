*About*

Jenni Ayala has been traveling from the west and east coasts with a few island hops in between. She has lived in New York, Miami, Berkeley, and San Fransisco. Starting as a seamstress at a bra company, she has continued her torrid love affair with lingerie throughout every incarnation of her careers, while doing professional makeup for lingerie shoots, styling, manicures and nail art, and more recently having her work reach the finals of "The Adorama Fashion Photographer of the Year" contest. She has taught yoga, cooked an all-vegan tailored menu for yoga retreats in Kaua'i, foraged, and is now living in Los Angeles and stepping back into her role as personal shopper and stylist.
